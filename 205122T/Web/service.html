Baby Delivery and Gynaecology

Aid allies has a dedicated unit for pregnancy & childbirth. Our expert panel of Gynecologist will guide you right from the beginning when you have decided to be a mother through the whole cycle of pregnancy till childbirth and beyond. With highly experienced specialists and care givers of the department, you can be rest assured you’re in safe hands.


The journey to motherhood is one of the special phases in women’s life. A woman’s body and soul undergoes lot of changes during pregnancy period. Women needs to take special care of them in this period. Our doctors are committed to provide world-class healthcare for expectant women. We also offer high standard reproductive health care services for women of all ages. Our expert gynecologists have dealt with many high risk pregnancy at helped parents deliver their bundle of joy. Having helped thousands of women taking care of their health and babies the dedicated baby delivery product “Maathru” is undoubtedly the most sought after maternity unit in the country.
